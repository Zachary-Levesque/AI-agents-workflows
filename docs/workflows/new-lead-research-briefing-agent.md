# New Lead Research Briefing Agent

## Goal

Monitor new lead rows, research the company, and draft an internal briefing for the lead team.

## Systems involved

- Google Sheets
- Tavily
- OpenAI
- Gmail

## Why it matters

This is a practical example of AI-assisted outbound intelligence with real operational value.

## Current strengths

- event-driven from a business system
- combines retrieval and summarization
- closes the loop with team communication

## Recommended next production upgrades

- add explicit source references in the email
- route low-signal results for manual review
- avoid auto-send for weak evidence
